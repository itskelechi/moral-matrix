# 🕺 MORALITY MATRIX: The Goodwill Challenge 🎲
### "Where Big Tech meets Big Ethics in a Retro-Rumble!"

Welcome to the **ITEC Ethics Challenge**, a high-stakes, 70s-inspired game show where college students navigate the murky waters of Information Technology and Engineering ethics. 

In this world, profit isn't the priority—**Goodwill** is the ultimate currency. Will you be a Corporate Crusader or a Startup Saint? Grab your polyester suit and let’s find out!

---

## 🎮 How to Play

1.  **Choose Your Field:** At the start of your turn, pick your sector: **Healthcare, Corporation, Government, or Startup**. 
    * *Pro Tip:* Every field starts with a secret Goodwill bonus. Choose wisely—some start with a "Public Trust" boost, while others start in the "Private Equity" basement!
2.  **Face the Dilemma:** You’ll be presented with a real-world ITEC ethical scenario. These aren't easy "yes or no" questions—they are the tough calls that define modern tech.
3.  **Make Your Move:** Pick the path you think is best. Your decision will immediately impact your **Goodwill Meter**. 
4.  **Win the Crowd:** The player who ends the game with the highest Goodwill score is crowned the **Ethics Icon**!

---

## 🛠️ Features

* **70s Retro Aesthetic:** Immersive styling featuring mustard yellow, burnt orange, and avocado green palettes.
* **Dynamic Scoring:** A hidden "Goodwill" multiplier system based on sector selection.
* **Educational Utility:** Designed specifically for college-level ITEC courses to spark debate on social impact and professional responsibility.
* **Single-File Simplicity:** No installation required! Just open the `.html` file in any modern web browser.

---

## 🚀 Quick Start

1.  **Download** the `index.html` file.
2.  **Open** the file in Chrome, Firefox, or Safari.
3.  **Compete** with your classmates to see who can build the most Goodwill!
